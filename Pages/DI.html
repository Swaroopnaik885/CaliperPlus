Detailing and Interior Cleaning

Detailing and Interior cleaning

Complete Care for Your Car — Inside & Out

Car detailing and interior cleaning go beyond a regular wash. It’s a deep, professional cleaning and restoration process designed to protect your vehicle, improve comfort, and keep it looking and feeling brand new.

Exterior Detailing — What We Do

1. Safe Wash & Decontamination

  • Foam wash and hand wash using paint-safe methods
  • Removal of tar, dirt, and surface contaminants

2. Paint Enhancement (Optional)

  • Light polishing to remove swirl marks and dullness
  • Restores gloss and paint clarity

3. Protection Layer

  • Wax, sealant, ceramic, or graphene coating options
  • Protects paint from UV rays, pollution, and weather damage

4. Final Finish

  • Exterior plastics, trims, and glass cleaned and dressed
  • Tires receive a clean, satin finish

Interior Cleaning — Deep & Hygienic

1. Vacuuming & Dust Removal

  • Seats, carpets, mats, dashboard, and vents thoroughly vacuumed

2. Upholstery & Seat Cleaning

  • Fabric seats: Deep shampoo cleaning
  • Leather seats: Gentle cleaning and conditioning

3. Dashboard & Panel Care

  • Non-greasy cleaning of dashboard, doors, and consoles
  • Restores natural finish without shine buildup

4. Stain & Odor Removal

  • Removal of food stains, spills, and bad odors
  • Leaves the cabin fresh and comfortable

5. Interior Protection (Optional)

  • Leather protection and conditioning
  • Fabric protection to resist future stains
