Paint Protection Film

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Invisible Armor for Your Vehicle

Paint Protection Film, commonly known as PPF, is a transparent, ultra-thin polyurethane film applied over your car’s paint to protect it from everyday damage. It acts like a clear shield that keeps your vehicle looking new for years.

What Does PPF Protect Against?

  • Stone Chips & Road Debris
  • Scratches & Swirl Marks
  • Bug Splatter & Bird Droppings
  • UV Rays & Paint Fading
  • Minor Abrasions & Stains

Key Benefits

Self-Healing Technology

Light scratches disappear with heat from the sun or warm water.

Crystal Clear Finish

Maintains the original paint color and gloss — completely invisible.

Long-Term Protection

High-quality films last 5–10 years with proper care.

UV & Chemical Resistance

Prevents discoloration and protects against harsh environmental elements.

Easy Maintenance

Less washing effort and safer cleaning.

*You can choose full-body coverage or high-impact areas only, depending on your needs and budget.*
