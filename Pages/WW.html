Wrap Work

Wrap Work

Style, Protect & Transform Your Car

A vehicle wrap is a high-quality vinyl film applied over your car’s original paint to change its look or promote a brand—without permanent paint changes. It’s a smart, reversible way to refresh your vehicle while protecting the original surface.

Step-by-Step Wrap Process

1. Surface Preparation

We start with a deep wash and detailing to remove dirt, grease, and residues. A clean surface ensures perfect adhesion and a flawless finish.

2. Design & Material Selection

Choose from solid colors, matte, gloss, satin, chrome, carbon fiber, or custom graphics. We help you pick the right vinyl based on style and durability.

3. Precision Application

Expert installers apply the wrap panel by panel using heat and professional tools. The vinyl conforms smoothly to curves, edges, and contours.

4. Heat Setting & Finishing

The wrap is heat-set to lock it in place. Edges are sealed for long-lasting hold and a factory-finish look.

5. Final Inspection

We inspect every detail to ensure there are no bubbles, wrinkles, or imperfections.

What a Vehicle Wrap Does for You

  • Instant Style Change – New look without repainting
  • Paint Protection – Shields from scratches, UV rays, and minor abrasions
  • Fully Reversible – Remove anytime to reveal original paint
  • Cost-Effective – More affordable than a full paint job
  • Customization – Endless colors, textures, and designs
