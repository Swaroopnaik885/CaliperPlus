Ceramic & Graphene Coatings

Ceramic Coating

Gloss. Protection. Durability.

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer infused with Silicon Dioxide
(SiO₂) that bonds chemically with the surface to form a hard,
transparent layer. It is not wax or polish — it becomes a
semi-permanent protective shield.

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer infused with Silicon Dioxide (SiO₂) that bonds chemically with the surface to form a hard, transparent layer. It is not wax or polish — it becomes a semi-permanent protective shield.

Hydrophobic Effect – Water beads and slides off easily
UV Protection – Prevents fading and oxidation
Chemical Resistance – Guards against bird droppings, acid rain, and stains
Enhanced Shine – Deep mirror-like gloss finish
Light Scratch Resistance – Reduces swirl marks and micro-scratches

Graphene Coating

Next-Generation Protection. Stronger. Smarter. Longer-Lasting.

Graphene coating is an advanced upgrade of ceramic coating
infused with Graphene nanoparticles — one of the strongest
materials known. It delivers superior durability, heat resistance,
and anti-static performance.

Graphene coating is an advanced upgrade of ceramic coating infused with Graphene nanoparticles — one of the strongest materials known. It delivers superior durability, heat resistance, and anti-static performance.

Superior Strength – Enhanced molecular structure
Anti-Static Surface – Repels dust and dirt buildup
High Heat Resistance – Reduces water spotting in hot climates
Extreme Hydrophobicity – Faster water beading and runoff
Longer Lifespan – Better performance in harsh conditions
